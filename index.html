Biography

Kazuki Tamura is a research assistant professor at Asian Growth Research Institute. His research interests include urban analysis, transport policy and spatial econometrics. He was born in Kyoto, grow up in Matsuyama and Studied urban planning at University of Tsukuba. After working as a researchrer at Tokyo and Fukuoka, he lives in Kitakyushu.
